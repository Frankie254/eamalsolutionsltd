About Us
Who we are

Eamal Solutions limited is a professional accredited employment agency in Kenya(Nairobi).We provide a level  of specialist services to an assorted echelon of businesses while achieving an industry leading retention rate of placements.

Mission

Connecting great companies with great employees and opening up opportunities for both, by providing the highest quality of outsourced manpower and human resource services.

Vision

To empower our clients,develop our relationship,our services and overall business together.

Our Services

Thank you for your inquiry about the services of EAMAL SOLUTIONS LIMITED. We would be happy to help you in your search for the perfect job. We specialize in connecting job seekers with employers that are looking for qualified individuals. Our services include resume writing and review, job postings, job search assistance,career counseling,passport application,ticketing and visa processing. We also provide resources to help job seekers prepare for interviews and negotiate salaries. Our team of experienced professionals is committed to helping you find the right job. Please feel free to contact us with any questions or to discuss your job search needs. 
Thank you again for considering us for your employment needs.
